WHAT'S HAPPENING

There's always something going on at Ditto's!

From weekly traditions to special occasions, join us for a good time. Check back often or follow us on social media for the latest updates!

Weekly Karaoke Night

Every Friday Night!

Time: 9:00 PM - Close

Warm up those vocal cords! It's your time to shine on the Ditto's stage. All singers welcome, from shower superstars to seasoned performers. Huge song selection!

No cover charge. Just bring your enthusiasm!

Saturday Meat Raffles

Every Saturday Afternoon!

Time: Starts at 2:00 PM

Feeling lucky? Join us for our popular Saturday meat raffles. High-quality cuts up for grabs every week. It's a St. Michael tradition!

Get here early to get your tickets!

Live Music & Entertainment

Select Saturday Nights & Special Occasions

We love to showcase local talent! Keep an eye on our schedule for upcoming live bands, acoustic sets, and other special entertainment.

Details for specific artists and dates will be posted here and on our social media.

Watch The Game Here!

With multiple screens, Ditto's is a great place to catch your favorite Minnesota sports teams and major sporting events. Enjoy the game with cold drinks and good company!

Special Community Events

Ditto's is proud to host and support various community events throughout the year, like the annual Chad Smith Ride for Life Bike Run and other local fundraisers and gatherings. We believe in giving back to the St. Michael community that supports us!

Stay tuned for announcements on upcoming special events!
