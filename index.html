WELCOME TO DITTO'S

Located in the first brick building ever built in St. Michael (1878!), Ditto's Bar has been serving up good times for generations. We're your go-to spot for a cold beer, classic cocktails, bar games, lively karaoke nights, and live music. Come on in, grab a drink, and become part of the Ditto's family!
