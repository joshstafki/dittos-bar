OUR STORY

More than just a bar – we're a piece of St. Michael history.

FROM 1878 TO TODAY

Ditto's Bar proudly stands in what was the very first brick building constructed in St. Michael, way back in 1878. Imagine the stories these walls could tell! Originally serving the burgeoning community in various capacities, it found its true calling as a place for locals to gather and unwind.

In 1988, Maurice “Ditto” J. Kilian took over Wayne's Bar, gave it a remodel, and the Ditto's Bar we know and love was born. He owned and ran it with passion until 2002, cementing its status as a local institution. While ownership may have changed hands since, the spirit of Ditto's – a welcoming, no-frills spot for everyone – remains strong.

THE DITTO'S VIBE

Step inside and you'll find a classic, casual dive bar atmosphere. We're not about pretension; we're about good company, cold drinks, and a good time. Whether you're here to shoot some pool, try your luck at bar games, belt out a tune on karaoke night, catch some live local music, or just watch the game with friends, you're always welcome at Ditto's.

We're a cornerstone of the St. Michael community, a place where familiar faces meet and new friends are made. Come experience the tradition!
